- About -

We are a team of students from the Technical University of Moldova who, in the context of the Project Based Learning, decided to develop a tool that could provide our citizens with an overview of the self-employed sector , thus motivating them to conduct independent activities in the field of small entrepreneurship.

Features:

  • Daily updated data collected as a result of automatic processing of more than 20 thousand ads from all over the country ;
  • Use of effective and popular methods of data analysis;
  • A visual demonstration of the most important market indicators.
